# Privacy Policy — Jansori 잔소리

**Last updated:** March 27, 2026

Jansori is a browser extension that provides posture, walk, and hydration reminders.

## Data Collection

Jansori does **not** collect, transmit, store on remote servers, or share any personal data whatsoever.

## Local Storage

Jansori stores the following data locally on your device using the browser's storage API:

- Your reminder preferences (which reminders are enabled, interval durations)
- Sound selection and volume settings
- Language preference (English or Korean)
- Custom uploaded sound files (stored as base64 data)

This data **never leaves your device**. It is not transmitted to any server, including BurgundyTable or any third party.

## Permissions

| Permission | Why It's Needed |
|------------|----------------|
| `alarms` | To schedule periodic reminders at your chosen intervals |
| `storage` | To save your preferences locally on your device |
| `offscreen` (Chrome only) | To play notification sounds (Chrome service workers cannot play audio directly) |
| `notifications` | To display reminder pop-up notifications |

## Third-Party Services

Jansori loads Google Fonts (Libre Baskerville, Noto Sans KR) for UI display. This is the only external network request made by the extension.

## Contact

For questions about this policy, visit [burgundytable.com](http://burgundytable.com).
